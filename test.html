Analytics/Informatics

Laboratories and Biotech businesses generate huge amounts of data. This type of business then integrates these systems with the complex systems required to operate such as LIMS, Robotics/Instrumentation and Quality Assurance Tracking applications. We have the unique ability to apply our technical expertise with our deep operational understanding of the information that users, managers and executives in these types of companies need to be highly productive. Whether looking at experimental outcomes, biotech production parameters or compliance data, Yahara is able to apply common analytics techniques on top of your biotech and laboratory data to deliver the information your organization needs to create great scientific workflows.

On the health sciences front, we primarily work with independent healthcare quality focused organizations that provide health quality reporting services on behalf of their clients or governmental agencies. We have proven that we can provide value in aggregating large payer, provider and laboratory result data streams to provide meaningful analytics. This information allows our partners to provide the information they need to optimize their processes and even apply for federal grants based upon the metrics they are able to collect.

Visualization integration – We understand that many times the information generated within science based organizations simply cannot be effectively visualized using bar charts and simple gauges, so we assist our clients in selecting the right visualization tool to fit with their specific needs, making sure they can make sense of it in a way the can share it with their organization.
